Knowledge Base

How do I create a support ticket?

Login to your account, open Dashboard and click Create Ticket.

How do I check ticket status?

Open My Tickets page to view all submitted tickets and their current status.

How do I reset my password?

Contact the administrator or support team to reset your account password.

How long does ticket resolution take?

Resolution time depends on ticket priority and issue complexity.

What do ticket statuses mean?

Open = Newly created ticket.
In Progress = Agent is working on it.
Resolved = Issue has been fixed.
